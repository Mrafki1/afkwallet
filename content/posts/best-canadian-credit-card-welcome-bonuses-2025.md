If you only apply for one or two cards a year, picking the right welcome bonus makes a massive difference. A well-timed application can net you $1,000–$2,500+ in value — without changing how you spend.

Here's a look at the strongest offers available to Canadians right now.

## 1. American Express Platinum Card — ~$2,200 first-year value

The Amex Platinum consistently sits at the top of the pile. The welcome bonus alone is worth over $1,000 when redeemed through Amex's transfer partners (Aeroplan, Avios, Flying Blue), and the card comes loaded with annual credits that chip away at the $799 fee.

Key perks on top of the bonus:
- $200 travel credit
- $200 dining credit
- Unlimited Priority Pass lounge access
- Fine Hotels & Resorts program

The MSR is $3,000 in the first 3 months — aggressive, but achievable if you time it around a big purchase.

**Best portal:** Apply via GCR for an extra $150 cash back on top of the welcome bonus.

## 2. American Express Cobalt Card — ~$1,200 first-year value

The Cobalt is the best everyday card in Canada, and the elevated welcome bonus makes it even more compelling as a first apply. You earn 5x Membership Rewards on food and drinks, which adds up fast if you eat out regularly.

The annual fee is only $155.88 ($12.99/month), and the monthly welcome bonus structure means you collect points in chunks over 12 months rather than all at once.

**Best portal:** GCR is currently offering the highest cash back for this card.

## 3. Scotiabank Passport Visa Infinite — ~$1,100 first-year value

The Passport VI is the best no-foreign-transaction-fee travel card in Canada. If you travel internationally at all, the 2.5% FX fee waiver alone can save you hundreds annually.

The welcome bonus is solid, and the card comes with 6 free Priority Pass lounge visits per year — a huge perk for occasional travellers who don't want to pay Amex Platinum fees.

**Best portal:** Check Frugal Flyer for current cash back rates.

## How to maximize your welcome bonus

A few things to keep in mind when applying:

**Time your application before a big purchase.** If you know you have a large expense coming (rent, insurance, flight, contractor), apply right before so you can hit the MSR without changing your spending habits.

**Use the best rebate portal.** Portals like GCR, Frugal Flyer, and Credit Card Genius pay you extra cash just for applying through their link. Always compare before clicking "Apply."

**Track your MSR deadline.** Most cards give you 3 months. Add the card to your ChurnCA dashboard immediately after applying so you never lose track.
